PROJECT OVERVIEW

Blending Sports with Technology

Blending the excitement of sports with technology, Benghazi Stadium in Libya has embraced digital transformation to elevate the fan experience.

Across the stadium, TOCHI outdoor digital signage displays deliver high visibility and exceptional durability, allowing visitors to access information instantly, follow live content, and enjoy a more immersive viewing experience.

With TOCHI, matchdays in Benghazi have become more engaging and dynamic — setting a modern standard for sports venues across the region.
